About Rapid Repair Recovery

The main aim is in the name - we aim to provide "RAPID" repair and maintenance services to customer vehicles such as cars, vans, trucks and trailers.

With over 15 years in the motor trade, we understand the key values of customer service when it comes to your vehicle. We aim to combat the long waiting times and shortage of vehicle mechanics by starting our own mobile and fleet maintenance service to keep your business's wheels turning.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and professional vehicle repair services with exceptional customer service. We believe in getting you back on the road as quickly as possible without compromising on quality.
